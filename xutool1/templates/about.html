About the Xu Initiative

This is a prototype data analysis tool using GDELT data to understand how frequently a subject is mentioned in the news in different countries. An user may search news stories with either a generally positive or negative tone. The result is a chloropleth map where the darker shaded countries/regions report a higher relative ratio of news stories that match the criteria. Only stories published within the last 24 hours are used for the analysis. The user may select a country and follow the embedded link to read a matching news story.
